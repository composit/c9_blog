about

Composition9 Design, LLC is Matt Rose. I am a professional web developer based in Fort Collins, Colorado. I work on a wide variety of projects, from informational web pages to internal information management systems. Feel free to send me your project proposals. If I do not think my skills are the right match for your project, I might be able to point you in the right direction.

I build highly accessible, highly functional web pages and browser-based information systems.

My focus is server-side scripting and standards-compliant web development. I mainly code using Ruby on Rails, MySQL, Javascript (AJAX), CSS and XHTML, and am well-versed in many other technologies, including, but not limited to: PHP, Apache, Mongrel, Crystal Reports, Linux, Subversion.

I work closely with graphic designers to build your site to your aesthetic specifications. We make it look great. Or if you have a graphic designer you would like to play the design role in your project, I work well in a team environment.
